Ropeworks National Training Center

In July 2006 we opened the Ropeworks National Training Center. The 7500 sq. ft. indoor facility, located minutes from the Reno/Tahoe International Airport, is North America’s premier purpose-built training facility for rope access, tower climbing and industrial rescue. The facility, perched above the Truckee River, serves as our Rope Access COE and our equipment distribution center. More about our National Training Center here.

Sterling Training Center

Sterling Ropes is a leading USA-based rope manufacture based in Biddeford, ME. Their manufacturing complex houses a training facility where we currently offer rope access courses on a semi-annual basis. Based on facility availability we also have the option to schedule additional courses for customers interested in reserving a full course for their team. See our course calendar for scheduled courses or contact us to inquire about booking a custom course at the Sterling Ropes Training Facility. training@ropeworks.com

  • Address: 26 Morin St, Biddeford Maine 04005
