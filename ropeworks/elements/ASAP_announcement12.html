Clarification to: The Back-up Device Dilemma


Friends, hello again.

We need to make a clarification to our Back-up Device communication. In the section highlighting our key conclusions, the second sentence of the second bullet point should read: "This practice, however, is likely to increase wear on the ASAP" and not "...to increase rope wear."

We have not identified any significant wear of ropes due to locking or engaging the ASAP device. Wear related to locking or engaging the device on rope is likely to be focused within the ASAP itself.
