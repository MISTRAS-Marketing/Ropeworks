MISTRAS Opens Ropeworks® Gulf Regional Rope Access Training Center

Construction of a new Ropeworks rope access training facility at MISTRAS’ Gulf Region training center is complete, with the first training courses going on this month. MISTRAS is the parent company of Ropeworks.

The training center located at the Gulf Regional Headquarters in La Porte, Texas is the fourth MISTRAS Ropeworks training center, with others located in Reno, NV, Anchorage, AK and one in South Wales, United Kingdom. The new location near Houston will be ideally situated to serve a fast-growing rope access market in the region.

“The training program is an essential component of our strong safety record. Offering the courses to the general public is also an exceptional way to support our industry, raise professional standards and develop relationships.” said Jan Holan, MISTRAS Ropeworks COE Manager.

Ropeworks has established itself as a leader in rope access training by investing into curriculum design, research, and instructor development.” said MISTRAS Ropeworks Training Manager, Chad Shearer.

The rope access training course involves classroom instruction, hands-on training in a purpose-built facility followed by a written and practical examination by a certified assessor. Good physical health, a positive work ethic, and strong team orientation are key attributes of excellent rope access team members.
